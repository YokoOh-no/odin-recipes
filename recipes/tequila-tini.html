Tequila-Tini

Description

The Tequila-Tini is a Fork and Fire original-classic cocktail. Notes of fresh citrus, caramel, and lime zest work together to fight the forces of mesquite smoke, and tequila spice, in the battle for your pallette"

Ingredients

  • .75oz Smoked OJ
  • 1.5oz Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 1.5oz Maestro Dobel Diamante Tequila
  • 1oz Agave
