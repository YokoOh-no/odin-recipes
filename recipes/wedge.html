How to make a Fork & Fire wedge salad.

Ingredients

  • One quarter head of iceburg lettuce
  • Two peices of Duroc bacon.
  • A quarter cup of gargazola bleu cheese
  • One Shallot,(thinly sliced).
  • 2ozblue cheese dressing.

Instructions

  1. 1.Place qquartered head of iceburg lettuce in center of plate.
  2. 2.Evenly pour homemade blue cheese dressing over lettuce.
  3. 3.Top blue cheese dressing, and lettuce with blue cheese crumbles.
  4. 4.Cut each peice of bacon twice, making 6 smaller peices of bacon. Add to top of salad
  5. 5.Add sliced shallots to the top of the salad
  6. 6.Enjoy!
